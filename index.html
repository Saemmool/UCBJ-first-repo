DUMPLING (Jiaozi)

Ingredients

  • Dumpling wrappers
  • Ground pork
  • Napa cabbage
  • Salt
  • Sesame oil
  • Green onion

Making dumplings

1. Chop the cabbage and marinate with or 10 minutes, then squeeze extra liquid out.

2. Carefully mix ground pork, cabbage and chopped green onions.

3. Add 25g of sesame oil and 10g of salt to the mixer, and the filling is done.

4. Put rib in boiling water for 10 seconds until there's no blood seen on the bone

5. Place some filling in the center of a dumpling wrapper, then dab water all the way around the outside edge of the wrapper.

6. Fold edges over and pinch them tightly, then fold a small portion of the top dumpling skin from two sides toward the center to create pleats. Or create any shape as you like!

Cooking

Heat a large pot of water to boiling and add dumplings in batches. When the dumplings start to float on top of the water, add 1/2 cup of cold water. The dumplings are ready to be served once the water boils again.
